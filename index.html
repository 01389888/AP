Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as an online interactive appendix as part of an applied project titled 'A Comparison of Formulations of the Volatility Surface'. The applied project has been completed as part of an MSc in Risk Management and Financial Engineering at Imperial College Business School.

The three models cosidered are:

  • the Libor Market Model (LMM)
  • the SABR model
  • a non-parametric local volatility model for swaptions.
The surfaces and other relevant figures for each of these models can be found from the dropdown menus above.

The applied project can be downloaded from here.
